About

Aliana Ramdass is an animator and illustrator based in New York. She was born and raised for eight years in Guyana. Aliana moved to Queens, NY in 2001.

Aliana collects or pins these images for future reference. She is also a media fanatic. She loves to consume all forms of media including film and television, which inspire many of her designs.

Her vast knowledge of film and television allows her to tap into any references that she may need, be it style, process and/or content.
