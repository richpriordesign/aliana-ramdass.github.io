Massacre of Innocence |
Woven Narrative

Massacre of Innocence is a gate-fold accordion book. The left side of the gate-fold book features text from William Golding's "Lord of the Flies" on the left of the spread and a visualization of a corresponding word from Leigh Fondakowski's "Stories from Jonestown" on the right; these words give some insight into the essence of the paragraph. The right side of the gate-fold book features text from Leigh Fondakowski's "Stories from Jonestown" on the left of the spread and a visualization of a corresponding word from William Golding's "Lord of the Flies" on the right.
