Chance Typography Website

This is an exploration of type and it's literal depiction. The content of each page has been hand coded so that the section divs take up the full height of the screen. Unfortunately, because some things would shift when the size was too large, I chose to constrain the width. To get to the top of the next section one just has to click on the die at the bottom of each div.
